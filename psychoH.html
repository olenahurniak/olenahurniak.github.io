DENTIFY the 5elements of Maslow’s "Hierarchy of Needs”

There are five elements of Maslow’s “Hierarchy of Needs”. They are: physiological needs, safety and security, love and belonging, self-esteem and self-actualization. All of them are needed for healthy and happy life. But I do not think that person starts to realize them one after another in the very severe order. That’s true that when you are hungry you cannot think about self-esteem. But what about people who fasting because they want to realize their self-actualization. And even if the person realizes some needs not in full, he or she can start to realize the other stage of this hierarchy. It can be doing simultaneously. Physiological needs include food, water, pain release and everything connected with our body. Safety and security needs are our feeling that we are safe having a shelter, that the other cannot making harm for us, knowing that someone protect us. Love and belonging needs are connected with the real being of humans. I think that this stage differentiate us from the animals. As animals have physiological needs as well. And they create groups for satisfying their needs in safety and security and grow up the next generation. And the next level of needs is more human. It is connected with love, friend, and society. Self-esteem needs are closely connected with love and belonging. As we are loved and recognized in the society or group we belong to, we automatically feels ourselves confident and approved. It is important for our psychological healthy I believe. Self-actualization needs are our wiliness to do everything we are doing. It is like a target point to which we are going. The hierarchy shows the equal importance of all stages of people needs, but I think that their importance is different in general and for every person in particular.
