Family

The family ... And at once, comfort, kindness, sincerity, and security come to mind. The family is the closest and most expensive people who want only the best of us. It is the family that will support and help in difficult situations, from the heart will be happy for success or victory. The family is the center of people in which you can be yourself, because you love what you are. In a real family, mutual respect, mutual help, and boundless love reign. Everyone plays a special role: mother's tenderness and affection always support the harmony in the family, thanks to the father's firm word you can feel confident, grandmother's or grandfather's wise thought will help to solve any situation. The true family has to dominate, above all, mutual respect, and then there will be peace, tenderness, care, love. Parents, be an example for your children! Friends

A friend is a support, even if he cannot help in a particular situation. It's enough that he is just next to me. But friendship needs not only to find, but also to try to save it, which is much harder. It's important to remember about a person constantly about events that are important to her, and not to turn to only when we themselves need help. After all, every person needs insight and understanding. A lot of people around me are with whom I communicate. But I can name just a few of them as a true friend. Why are they? Because they are real, dear to me, because our admiration is very similar, we love the same thing. I am very pleased to spend time with them, and I bored when they are not near.
