Which would you choose: A stressful job with a very high pay or a relaxing job with considerably low pay? Why?

I will choose the job I will like more. The same job can be stressful for one and relaxing for other, or at least less stressful. The reason is what we are ready for. I could choose the stressful job with a very high pay if it will answer my demands and preference. The main question is what will be my stress response to the stressors of this job. Besides when you go to some job for the first time it is stress itself. Everything is new for you no matter what experience you had before. Understanding this may decrease the stress influence. The right thought is: “I am ready that Everything will be new. I am ready to look like the child for the first time. I am ready to learn.” I believe that will be enough for the first time to reduce the negative stress influence to a person. And if the job is always stressful, for example, if you must always meet new people, work with them and you do not know how they react; or the job is always changing and you cannot be sure what to do than the person must find the own ways to cope with this stress. In any way it depends on our way to respond the stressors. The most stressful job for me is to work always with the new people. It is interesting for sure, but when you have to meet every day the new ones, it may be stressful. I feel responsibility for the communication with everyone I speak with, so I cannot be sure that the person feels comfortable having the small talk with me. I need more time to communicate and to get know the person. That is the kind of job that will be the most stressful for me. To work at it I must change my attitude to people. I think I could do it but now I really do not want to)
