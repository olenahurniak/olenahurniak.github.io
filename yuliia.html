Yuliia Zavidniuk

Yuliia is a small pretty girl. She is just one year old. She likes to discover the world very much. Every day she finds out something new. Books are her favourite toys. Yuliia brings them to her father and mother and points to different things with her finger. Her parents tell her what it is and she likes such play very much. Besides if tell her show something she can already find it in the book. Yuliia does not speak yet, but she is pronouncing some syllabus all the time. It looks like the foreigner tries to talk to you but you do not understand this language. The girl is very energetic and likes to walk, run and dance. Many things she wants to do by herself, for example open the boxes, draw with the chalk, eat, take or put something. She always delightful smiles when sees the other children and wants to play with them. In general, Yuliia is very positive, active and smart toddler.

Written by

Kateryna Zavidniuk, March 04, 2019
