Top cities in Canada:

Vancouver

One of the cities with the highest rating on the quality of life in the world. What makes it so special? First of all its geographical position. The city occupies two shores of the big river Burrard. From the northern part it is surrounded by a mountain range, which protects from winds and provides a mild climate (in winter, the temperature drops only to 0 ° C). In addition, Vancouver is well-designed in terms of urbanism: a competent and developed system of public transport, a large number of green areas, cleanliness of the streets and security. In good weather townspeople spend time on numerous beaches and parks. In the doun town you will find restaurants, live music bars, pubs, museums, galleries, author's boutiques - everything you need for an interesting cultural life. Due to the large amount of entertainment Vancouver is sometimes called Manhattan in the mountains. Interesting detail: It is believed that Vancouver has the best places in the coffeeshops with great choices of varieties and types of cooking. I think it's a good reason to visit the city for fans of this drink.

Montreal

Montreal is Canada's most European city. This is the provincial student center, with two well-known universities: McGill University and Concordia University. There are many young people in the city and, of course, a huge number of bars, discos and music clubs with discriminatory prices. Concerts, live music is one of the main entertainments of the city's residents. In the Quartier des spectacles district, festivals and performances by Canadian and world artists take place every weekend. For music lovers, I want to note that Leonard Cohen, Arcade Fire, Sam Roberts, Rufus Wainwright, Oscar Peterson, Celine Dion are all from Montreal.

Toronto

Economic center of the country. In my opinion, it is similar to all the great metropolises of the world combined: noisy, with huge crowds hurry people, crowded with public transport, restaurants and shops. Why do I advise you to visit the city? First of all, to see business in Canada with skyscrapers, endless condominium towers, modern architecture of business centers

Victoria

It is considered to be one of the most beautiful cities in Canada. The central part includes the Old Town with buildings of the XIX century. In good weather, it's worthwhile to take a stroll through the old inner city port of Inner Harbor and take a ride on a small water taxi. When visiting it is worth paying attention to the parliament building on the shore of the picturesque embankment, executed in the style of neo-baroque. Hotel Empress Hotel, reminiscent of the castle, is one of the symbols of the city and famous for its guests, for example, the Queen Elizabeth was resting with her husband George.
