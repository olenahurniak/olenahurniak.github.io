Children

Children are our future

In the whole world, almost every family grows children. And the future of our planet depends on what they will do. Children will build cities and factories, new highways, grow crops, develop a culture. How will today's children do it - depends on the parents, the family in which they live, from the adults surrounding them. It is necessary that not only parents but all adults should see in every child a person of the future. And for this they must, first of all, respect the little personality, worry about her health, education, personal example to bring her honesty and justice. Children need to know well the history of their ancestry and their homeland to be proud of the successes of their ancestors and not to repeat their mistakes.


Mark, Arina, Emma, Lily, Yuliia, Sandra

They are children of the world. They have roots in Ukraine, Armenia, Russia, the USA. They have a nice parents, nice families. They are our future generation. We have the opportunity to show these children that we need them. We need them healthy, intelligent, strong. And that we will help them to become such, because it depends on us, how they grow up.

Written by

Olena Gurniak, March 08, 2019
